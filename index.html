OPERA

Me and my sister went to the an Italian opera called 'LA BOHEME'. March 16,2023

La Boheme is a work consisting of stories written by Henri Muger, with compositions prepared by Giacomo Puccini. This work, which tells the story of 4 friends (poet Rodolfo, painter Marcello, philosopher Colline and musician Schaunard) living a Bohemian life, is rich in acting, decorations and It was a great show with both stories and arias.

ANTALYA

Me and my friends went to Antalya for summer vacation. July 17, 2023

In my opinion, one of the most beautiful places in Antalya is Düden Waterfall. Besides the refreshing splashes of water in the middle of the Antalya heat, its nature was fascinating. I remember feeling very peaceful at this point where blue meets green. Apart from that, my trip to Antalya was one of the unforgettable memories of my life. I would also like to add that the best part of this vacation was that it was with my best friend Beyza.

ANITKABIR

I visited our great leader Mustafa Kemal Atatürk on his death anniversary. November 10, 2023

On that day, thousands of people gathered at Ankara's Kızılay Square to observe a minute of silence, commemorating our Great Leader. At 9:05, life stopped, but the only thing that didn't stop at that moment was our tears. I had goosebumps and, as a Turkish child, I once again realized the value of being born as such. I express my respect and gratitude to our Chief Teacher, Atatürk, who brought us to these days. 'Ne mutlu Türk'üm Diyene!'
